Programmer's Thinking Pub

PTP (Programmer's Thinking Pub) is a skype group dedicated to providing developers and programmers with a general chat to share their ideas, receive feedback on concepts or just chat about programming in general.

  • No spamming
  • Keep messages in english
  • Keep the overall topic on development & programming
  • Keep opinions on other's friendly
  • Do not add members without permission
  • Do not re-add members that have left or been removed
  • Do not start heavily controversial topics that could cause issues
  • Do not advertise paid services
  • Do not judge another developer's programming capability
  • Skype bots are subject to the same rules as normal members
